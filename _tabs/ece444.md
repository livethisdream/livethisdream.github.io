<p class="course-tagline" style="color:#666;margin-top:-0.5em;">Antennas, Phased Arrays, and Radar Systems &middot; Fall 2026</p>

This course progresses from fundamental electromagnetics and antenna theory
through antenna measurement, phased-array beamforming with the **ADALM-PHASER**
platform, and **FMCW radar** concepts. Theory and hands-on labs culminate in a
capstone project integrating beamforming and radar signal processing.
